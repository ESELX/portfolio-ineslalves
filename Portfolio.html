Portfolio work

Graphic Design

Cultural magazine developed for Graphic Design. This magazine was made with the purpose of learning how to use InDesign, and how to print a magazine, as well as how to organize contents included in the index. In this project I personally learned how to use the program pretended, and how to use Design in order to appeal the client. Having an envisionment of how the work place/flow might in the future, to work as a Designer.

3D Representation

Blender project, for 3D representation. The pupose of this project was to recreate Charmander, a character from the Pokemon series, in order to learn the bases of Blender and explore the tools of this program. In this project I learned how to ise cycles Blender and how model a simple character, as well as how to adapt render to the project made so that it becomes easier to work in the character, individually.

Painting: Erro

Academic assignment for Painting. The purpose was to create a painting based on the theme: Mistake. Complying with the rules of an installation painting, the subtopic I developed is about Environmental awareness. In this, it as made an oil painitng with three faces, each covered by on kind of landscape, urban or nature. This way, the purpose was to adapt the meaning of "The Error" to a concept of Human Error, as the humanity keeps trying not to see the problems it is causing to the planet, blecause of polution, plastic use, global warming and water use, and the living species disappearing .

Sculpture

Academic assignment for Sculpture. The purpose was to product a cement model of our hand, holding stones in it, to create a concept related to violence. For this project I used the concept of violence against Arab women.

Product Design

Project made for a Product Design assignment.The purpuse was to create a razor blade that had something new or different than the remaining others. My concept is on a curved razor blade, that reaches the majority of the leg with it comes to depilation. The razor set has three kinds of blade, one for the chest, one for the face, and one for the legs. This group project pretended to create a sample razor that would be usefull for more that one part of the body, and still keeping the hygiene as a priority.

Interaction Design

App developed for Interaction Design. The Application developed had the intention of teaching the students how to construct the organization of the inside of an App, looking to it s a client. This way, it was learned how to correcly make and associate an App to an object (razor blade project) in the User prespective. Mainly gain the capacity of looking to design as a way of communication with the technologie world, as well as in a commercial way.
