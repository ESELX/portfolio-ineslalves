    Welcome!

    Hello! I am Inês, a 22 year old Art student from Torres Vedras, Portugal. On this website I will be showing some of my work, on Arts and Design. Mainly Academic. My interests include drawing, painting, design, 3D modeling and video editing.

    Graphic design

    Cultural magazine

    Academic project developed for graphic design, with the purpuse of creating a cultural magazine, containing real programming, events and news. In order to practice for possible clients.

    3D Representation

    Charmander

    Blender project, for 3D representation. The pupose would be to recreate Charmander, a character from the Pokemon series, in order to learn the bases of Blender and explore the tools of this program.

    Painting

    Painting: O Erro

    Academic assignment for Painting. The purpose was to create a painting based on the theme: Mistake. Complying with the rules of an installation painting, the subtopic I developed is about Environmental awareness.

    Sculpture

    Stones in the hand

    Academic assignment for Sculpture. The purpose was to product a cement model of our hand, holding stones in it, to create a concept related to violence. For this project I used the concept of violence against Arab women.

    Product design

    Razor blade

    Project made for a Product Design assignment.The purpuse was to create a razor blade that had something new or different than the remaining others. My concept is on a curved razor blade, that reaches the majority of the leg with it comes to depilation. The razor set has three kinds of blade.

    Interaction design

    Razor blade App

    Project made for an Interaction Design assignment. From the Razor Blade project, the purpose would be to create a fully funcional application based on the razor brand, in order to please the user. In this case the brand was Philips and the main colors used were green, white and black, as the brand.
